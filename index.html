About Us

Abhiyan is an AI-powered chatbot developed to streamline access to government schemes, especially for rural users who may lack technical knowledge. Its core purpose is to simplify data retrieval, enhancing in formation accessibility and bridging the digital divide.

How to Reach Abhiyan AI?

Step 1:

Login to your account

Login with your username and password if already signed in. Else Sign in yourself with the link in the home page.

Step 2:

Add your Details for Personalized Recommendations

Once logged in Abhiyan will then ask you your details such as your age, income, occupation, and location. On submitting we provide you Recommendations on various Government Schemes.

Step 3:

Interact with our AI chatbot for more details

Click on the recommended schemes or search for any, your Abhiyan Information Assistant will help you resolve your queries.
